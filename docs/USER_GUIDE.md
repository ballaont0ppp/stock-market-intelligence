## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the registration page
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, and numbers)
4. Enter your full name
5. Click "Register"
6. You'll be automatically logged in with a starting balance of $100,000 in virtual funds

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Login"
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard displays:
- **Wallet Balance**: Available cash for trading
- **Portfolio Value**: Total value of your stock holdings
- **Total Net Worth**: Combined wallet and portfolio value
- **Recent Activity**: Your latest transactions
- **Top Holdings**: Your best-performing stocks
- **Quick Predict**: Fast access to stock predictions

## Account Management

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Click on your name in the top navigation
2. Select "Profile"
3. View your account information and trading statistics

### Updating Your Profile

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Update your information:
   - Full Name
   - Risk Tolerance (Conservative, Moderate, Aggressive)
   - Investment Goals
   - Preferred Sectors
   - Notification Preferences
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password
5. Confirm your new password
6. Click "Update Password"

## Portfolio Management

### Viewing Your Portfolio

1. Click "Portfolio" in the navigation menu
2. View your current holdings with:
   - Stock symbol and company name
   - Quantity owned
   - Average purchase price
   - Current market price
   - Total value
   - Unrealized gain/loss
   - Percentage change

### Managing Your Wallet

1. Click "Portfolio" → "Wallet"
2. View your current balance and transaction history

**Depositing Funds**:
1. Click "Deposit Funds"
2. Enter the amount (maximum $1,000,000 per deposit)
3. Add a description (optional)
4. Click "Deposit"

**Withdrawing Funds**:
1. Click "Withdraw Funds"
2. Enter the amount (cannot exceed current balance)
3. Add a description (optional)
4. Click "Withdraw"

### Portfolio Analytics

Your portfolio page shows:
- **Total Invested**: Amount spent on stock purchases
- **Current Value**: Current market value of holdings
- **Total Gain/Loss**: Overall profit or loss
- **Return Percentage**: Performance as a percentage
- **Sector Allocation**: Distribution across different sectors

## Trading Stocks

### Buying Stocks

1. Click "Orders" in the navigation menu
2. Click "Buy Stock" or use the quick buy form
3. Enter the stock symbol (e.g., AAPL, GOOGL, MSFT)
   - Use the autocomplete feature to search for stocks
4. Enter the quantity of shares
5. Review the order preview:
   - Current price per share
   - Total cost
   - Commission fee (0.1% of transaction value)
6. Click "Place Buy Order"
7. Confirm the order
8. You'll receive a confirmation message with order details

**Important Notes**:
- You must have sufficient funds in your wallet
- Commission fee is 0.1% of the transaction value
- Orders are executed at current market price
- Maximum 1,000,000 shares per order

### Selling Stocks

1. Click "Orders" in the navigation menu
2. Click "Sell Stock"
3. Select the stock from your holdings
4. Enter the quantity to sell (cannot exceed owned shares)
5. Review the order preview:
   - Current price per share
   - Gross proceeds
   - Commission fee (0.1% of transaction value)
   - Net proceeds
   - Realized gain/loss
6. Click "Place Sell Order"
7. Confirm the order
8. You'll receive a confirmation message with order details

**Important Notes**:
- You can only sell stocks you own
- Commission fee is 0.1% of the transaction value
- Realized gain/loss is calculated automatically
- Proceeds are added to your wallet immediately

### Viewing Order History

1. Click "Orders" in the navigation menu
2. View all your past orders with:
   - Date and time
   - Stock symbol
   - Order type (BUY/SELL)
   - Quantity
   - Price per share
   - Total amount
   - Status (COMPLETED/FAILED/PENDING)

**Filtering Orders**:
- Filter by date range
- Filter by order type (BUY/SELL/ALL)
- Filter by stock symbol
- Filter by status

### Transaction History

1. Click "Orders" → "Transactions"
2. View detailed transaction history:
   - All wallet and trading transactions
   - Deposits and withdrawals
   - Commission fees
   - Dividend payments

## Stock Predictions

### Getting Stock Predictions

1. Click "Dashboard" or "Predict" in the navigation
2. Enter a stock symbol in the prediction form
3. Select prediction models (ARIMA, LSTM, Linear Regression)
4. Click "Predict"

### Understanding Predictions

The prediction results show:

**Price Predictions**:
- **ARIMA Model**: Time series forecasting
- **LSTM Model**: Deep learning neural network
- **Linear Regression**: Statistical regression
- Each model shows predicted price and error margin

**Sentiment Analysis**:
- Overall sentiment (Positive/Negative/Neutral)
- Polarity score (-1 to +1)
- Distribution of positive, negative, and neutral tweets
- Sample tweets from Twitter

**Recommendation**:
- Action: BUY, SELL, or HOLD
- Confidence level: HIGH, MODERATE, or LOW
- Reasoning based on predictions and sentiment

**Visualizations**:
- Historical price trends
- Model predictions vs actual prices
- Sentiment distribution pie chart
- 7-day price forecast

### Using Predictions for Trading

1. Review all three model predictions
2. Check sentiment analysis
3. Consider the recommendation
4. Make your trading decision
5. Click "Buy" or "Sell" directly from the prediction page

## Reports and Analytics

### Transaction Reports

1. Click "Reports" in the navigation menu
2. Select "Transaction Report"
3. Choose date range
4. Click "Generate Report"

The report includes:
- All transactions in the period
- Total buy amount
- Total sell amount
- Total commissions paid
- Net profit/loss

### Billing Reports

1. Click "Reports" → "Billing Report"
2. Select month and year
3. Click "Generate Report"

The report shows:
- Transaction count and volume
- Commission breakdown by type
- Total fees paid
- Monthly summary

### Performance Reports

1. Click "Reports" → "Performance Report"
2. Select time period
3. Click "Generate Report"

The report includes:
- Portfolio value over time
- Returns compared to benchmarks
- Best performing stocks
- Worst performing stocks
- Sector performance

### Exporting Reports

1. Generate any report
2. Click "Export to CSV" or "Export to PDF"
3. Save the file to your computer

## Notifications

### Viewing Notifications

- **Transaction Notifications**: Order confirmations

### Managing Notifications

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Update notification preferences:
   - Email notifications
   - Transaction alerts
   - Dividend alerts
   - Price movement alerts
3. Click "Save Preferences"

### Marking Notifications as Read

- Click on a notification to mark it as read
- Click "Mark All as Read" to clear all notifications

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Is this real money?**
A: No, this is a simulation platform using virtual funds. No real money is involved.

**Q: How much virtual money do I start with?**
A: Every new account starts with $100,000 in virtual funds.

**Q: Can I add more virtual funds?**
A: Yes, you can deposit additional virtual funds through the Wallet page (maximum $1,000,000 per deposit).

### Trading Questions

**Q: What are the trading fees?**
A: Commission fee is 0.1% of the transaction value for both buy and sell orders.

**Q: When are orders executed?**
A: Orders are executed immediately at the current market price.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**
A: Once an order is placed, it's executed immediately and cannot be cancelled.

**Q: What happens if I don't have enough funds?**
A: The order will fail with an "Insufficient funds" error message.

**Q: Can I sell stocks I don't own (short selling)?**
A: No, you can only sell stocks you currently own in your portfolio.

### Stock Data Questions

**Q: Where does the stock data come from?**
A: Stock prices are fetched from Yahoo Finance (yfinance API).

**Q: How often are prices updated?**
A: Prices are updated every 15 minutes during market hours and daily after market close.

**Q: Are the predictions accurate?**
A: Predictions are based on historical data and ML models. They should be used as guidance, not guarantees.

### Dividend Questions

**Q: Do I receive dividends?**
A: Yes, if you own stocks that pay dividends, you'll automatically receive dividend payments.

**Q: When are dividends paid?**
A: Dividends are processed daily at 4:00 PM EST on the payment date.

**Q: Where do dividend payments go?**
A: Dividend payments are automatically added to your wallet balance.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest 2 versions of each).

**Q: Is the platform mobile-friendly?**
A: Yes, the platform is fully responsive and works on tablets and smartphones.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Contact support through the help section or email support@example.com.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use industry-standard security measures including password hashing, HTTPS encryption, and secure session management.

### Account Questions

**Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, update your email in the Profile settings.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support to request account deletion.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Each user should have only one account per email address.
