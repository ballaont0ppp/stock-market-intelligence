# Usability Testing Consent Form

## Stock Portfolio Management Platform - Usability Study

### Purpose of the Study

You are being asked to participate in a usability study for a stock portfolio management platform. The purpose of this study is to evaluate the user experience and identify areas for improvement. Your participation will help us create a better product.

### What You Will Do

During this session, you will:
- Complete tasks on the platform (e.g., register, buy stocks, view portfolio)
- Share your thoughts aloud as you navigate
- Answer questions about your experience
- Complete a brief satisfaction survey

The session will last approximately **60 minutes**.

### Recording and Data Collection

With your permission, we will:
- Record your screen during the session
- Record audio of your comments
- Take notes on your interactions
- Collect survey responses

**All recordings and data will be used solely for research purposes.**

### Privacy and Confidentiality

- Your identity will be kept confidential
- You will be assigned a participant ID
- Your name will not be associated with the data
- Recordings will be stored securely
- Only the research team will have access to the data
- Data will be deleted after analysis is complete (within 6 months)

### Voluntary Participation

- Your participation is completely voluntary
- You may withdraw at any time without penalty
- You may skip any questions you don't want to answer
- You may request that your data be deleted

### Risks and Benefits

**Risks:**
- Minimal risk involved
- No personal or financial information will be collected
- You will use test accounts, not real money
